Dr. Luis Rios, Fission Bio's Co-Founder and Principal Investigator, made a crucial breakthrough by identifying the specific binding site – termed SWAG (switch I-adjacent groove) – where P110 interferes with the Fis1 interaction.

Building on years of P110 research, his team developed SC9, a small molecule that mimics P110's protective effects while overcoming peptide limitations (Rios et al., 2023).

# Community-Driven Development

Promising early-stage research often struggles to secure funding, despite its scientific merit.

Cerebrum DAO addresses this bottleneck through IP-Token funding. The community funded Fission Bio's initial $200,000 development phase, then tokenized the intellectual property through MITY tokens on the @Molecule_sci platform, raising an additional $188,000 for lead identification and in-vivo testing.
