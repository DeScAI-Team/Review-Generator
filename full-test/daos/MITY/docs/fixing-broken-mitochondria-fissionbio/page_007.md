*   **Oral delivery:** Pills are more convenient than injections and improve patient compliance

Most importantly, these compounds maintain P110's unique property of preventing pathological Drp1–Fis1 interaction without suppressing normal immune function or interfering with healthy mitochondrial dynamics.

This sets them apart from broad anti-inflammatory drugs that can compromise the body's ability to fight infections.

FissionBio's strategy is to develop a "pipeline in a pill" because the same mechanism can potentially treat multiple neurodegenerative conditions. Rather than developing separate drugs for each disease, they're targeting the common mitochondrial dysfunction that underlies them.

# How Cerebrum DAO Accelerates Scientific Discovery

Bringing new medicines to patients is notoriously difficult and expensive. Most promising research dies in the early stages, not because the science is bad, but because it can't attract the funding needed to prove its potential.

Traditional investors typically wait for later-stage data before committing resources, creating a gap that can stall breakthrough treatments for years. This gap is commonly referred to as the “Valley of Death,” or the translation gap.

Cerebrum DAO, a community-driven Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) focused on ending brain diseases, addresses this problem by enabling individuals to fund promising scientific research directly. DAOs focused on Life Sciences research are referred to as “BioDAOs.”

We committed $200,000 to support FissionBio’s initial development work, with an additional $200,000 raised from the community through the MITY IP-NFT crowdsale round, demonstrating the potential of crowdfunding science through a DAO.

But this isn’t just about cash inflows. BioDAOs offer several compelling advantages for scientific development that traditional funding mechanisms cannot match:

*   **Community expertise:** Members bring diverse backgrounds, including scientists, clinicians, patients, and industry professionals
*   **Faster decision-making:** No bureaucratic delays or lengthy committee approvals that can slow progress
