# Percepta Human Trial Approved by the IRB

## "Percepta for Cognitive Optimization: A 6-Month Randomized Controlled Trial with Neurocognitive, Wearable, and Participant-Reported Outcomes in Adults with Mild Cognitive Impairment"

We're very excited to announce that we've secured Institutional Review Board ("IRB") approval for our Percepta brain health human trial.

The IRB has approved a 6-month, double-blinded, decentralized, randomized placebo-controlled trial. This is the world's first tokenized brain health supplement to enter human trials and represents a significant milestone for DeSci and brain health.

We've been working towards this milestone since October 2024, when we tokenized Percepta as the Neuron_Percepta Intellectual Property Non-Fungible Token ("IP-NFT"). We subsequently crowd-funded over $500,000 (127.7 ETH) from brain health enthusiasts through the CLAW Intellectual Property Token ("IPT") to fund this study.

### The Trial

The trial design includes:

*   Neurocognitive assessments tracking memory, processing speed, and cognitive function
*   Wearable data integration for continuous health monitoring
*   P-tau 217 biomarker tracking – the leading blood-based biomarker for cognitive decline

### The Journey So Far

Percepta’s path to human trials began with laboratory research into PTI-00703® Cat’s Claw and MemorTea® oolong tea extract as potential neuroprotective compounds.
