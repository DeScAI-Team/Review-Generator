# Study Population

Inclusion criteria include adults aged 40 to 85, self-reported MCI (MoCA <25), ownership of an Oura Ring, and commitment to study procedures. Exclusion criteria include use of cognitive-affected medications, neurological or psychiatric disorders, allergies to ingredients, pregnancy, and APOE-4 allele carrier status. Participants must maintain stable lifestyles (diet, alcohol, substance use, physical activity, sleep) during the study.

# Safety and Monitoring

Safety will be monitored through continuous wearable device data, eligibility screening, and adverse event (AE) reporting. Participants will report AEs via a mobile application or email, and all AEs will be assessed for severity and relationship to the intervention. Serious adverse events (SAEs) will be reported to the IRB and sponsor within 48 hours. Participants will be provided compensation for completing the study.

# Scientific Rationale

The rationale for Percepta is based on the neuroprotective properties of cat's claw and oolong tea extract, which have shown potential in preclinical studies to mitigate neuroinflammation, oxidative stress, and protein aggregation. The randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled design ensures a rigorous evaluation of Percepta's efficacy and safety.

# Expected Outcomes

This study aims to provide foundational data on Percepta's safety, tolerability, and potential cognitive benefits. If successful, findings could inform future research and contribute to the development of accessible, natural interventions for individuals experiencing cognitive decline.
