# Milestone 1 Overview

## Background

MCI represents a critical stage between normal aging and dementia, with limited effective interventions. Percepta offers a novel, multi-faceted approach by leveraging the neuroprotective properties of its ingredients, which have shown promise in preclinical studies for reducing neuroinflammation, oxidative stress, and amyloid-beta plaque aggregation. Existing treatments for MCI, such as lecanemab and donanemab, have limitations in accessibility, cost, and safety, making alternative interventions necessary.

## Study Design

One hundred fifty-four participants aged 40 to 85 with self-reported MCI will be recruited and randomized 1:1 into either the Percepta or placebo group. The study will be conducted remotely, with participants receiving supplements at home and completing assessments via a mobile application and wearable devices (Oura Ring). Cognitive performance will be measured using the digital Montreal Cognitive Assessment (MoCA), wearable biomarker metrics (sleep quality, heart rate variability), and the Brain Health Index (BHI). An optional sub-study may assess blood biomarkers (tau protein and amyloid beta ratios). Interim analyses will be conducted at 3 months, with potential extensions to 9 and 12 months based on initial findings.

## Intervention

The active intervention is 750mg (2 capsules) of Percepta taken orally once daily. The placebo group will receive identical-looking capsules without active ingredients. Adherence will be tracked via a mobile application and wearable device data. Participants must maintain at least 80% adherence for per-protocol analysis and 95% for intention-to-treat analysis.

## Objectives and Endpoints

The primary objective is to determine if Percepta supplementation leads to significant improvements in cognitive performance as measured by the digital MoCA. Secondary objectives include improvements in wearable device biomarker metrics and BHI scores. Exploratory objectives may involve assessing blood biomarkers. Endpoints will be assessed at baseline, 3 months, and 6 months, with potential extensions to 9 and 12 months.
