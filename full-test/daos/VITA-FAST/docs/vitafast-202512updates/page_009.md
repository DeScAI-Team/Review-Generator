**Key foundational publications from Korolchuk Lab:**

* Kataura T et al. (2024) "Targeting the autophagy-NAD axis protects against cell death in Niemann-Pick type C1 disease models" – Cell Death & Disease. Demonstrates NAD preservation via autophagy in NPC disease models.
* Kataura T et al. (2022) "Autophagy promotes cell survival by maintaining NAD levels" – Developmental Cell. Establishes evolutionarily conserved role of autophagy in NAD preservation.
* Wilson N et al. (2023) "The autophagy-NAD axis in longevity and disease" – Trends in Cell Biology. Review of autophagy-NAD crosstalk and therapeutic implications.
* Sedlackova L et al. (2023) "Metabolic function of autophagy is essential for cell survival" – Autophagy. Commentary on autophagy-NAD connection and therapeutic targets.
* Sedlackova L & Korolchuk VI (2020) "The crosstalk of NAD, ROS and autophagy in cellular health and ageing" – Biogerontology. Review of NAD-ROS-autophagy interplay in aging.
* Sun C et al. (2023) "NAD depletion mediates cytotoxicity in human neurons with autophagy deficiency" – Cell Reports. Human neuronal validation of autophagy-NAD axis.

**Target validation:**

* Autophagy dysfunction is well-established in NPC, neurodegeneration, and aging
* NAD depletion in autophagy deficiency validated across yeast, mouse, and human models
* Pharmacological and genetic interventions targeting this cascade improve survival in autophagy-deficient cells
