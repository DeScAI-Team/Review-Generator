# MANDATORY INFORMATION

## Project Overview & Scientific Foundation

**Project Title:** VitaFAST
Brief, descriptive name for the therapeutic program

**Therapeutic Concept Summary:** Discovery and development of small-molecule activators of autophagy that rescue lysosomal dysfunction and cellular homeostasis. Compounds are identified via high-throughput phenotypic screening in autophagy-deficient cell models and optimized as first-in-class autophagy activators for aging and lysosomal storage disorders.

**Target Indication:** Longevity, Niemann-Pick type C (NPC), and other lysosomal storage disorders

**Patient Population Size:** Estimated number of patients who could benefit from the therapy

The VitaFAST program addresses multiple patient populations across its target indications:

*   **Niemann-Pick type C (NPC):** Ultra-rare genetic disorder with an estimated prevalence of ~1 per 100,000-120,000 live births globally. Diagnosed prevalent cases estimated at ~787 patients in the 7 major markets (2022), with ~335 in the US alone. Total NPC therapeutics market valued at ~$110-140 million (2024), projected to reach ~$210-260 million by 2032.
*   **Lysosomal storage disorders (broader category):** Estimated frequency of ~1 in 5,000 live births collectively. Global LSD therapeutics market valued at ~$9-11 billion (2025), growing at 4.5-8.4% CAGR to reach ~$13-20 billion by 2032-2034.
*   **Longevity/aging population:** Universal applicability – the entire aging population experiences autophagy decline. Global longevity and anti-aging drugs market estimated at $8.9 billion (2023), projected to reach $24.5 billion by 2032 (11.8% CAGR).
*   **Broader neurodegeneration:** Alzheimer's disease (50+ million patients globally), Parkinson's disease (10+ million globally) – conditions where autophagy dysfunction plays a key pathological role.
