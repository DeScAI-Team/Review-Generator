**Categorical Separation:** The DUNA and LLC have distinct legal purposes and rights. This separation ensures:

*   **DUNA = Membership:** Token holders are DAO members with governance voice but no automatic equity rights
*   **LLC = Ownership:** Shareholders hold equity with economic rights via separate agreements
*   **Tokens ≠ Securities:** Utility/commodity classification maintained
*   **Equity = Securities:** Issued only through compliant exemptions

## 4. Treasury Transfer to LLC

Transfer VITA-FAST treasury funds to VitaFast LLC:

*   Funds derived from IP-NFT fractionalization and token sales
*   LLC treasury enables traditional banking relationships for operational needs
*   On-chain transparency maintained through regular reporting
*   Funds remain dedicated to VITA-FAST purposes per Whitepaper and Assignment Agreements

## 5. Equity Financing Pathways

The LLC structure enables compliant equity financing through two pathways:

### Community Member Route:

Token holder → Voluntary token lock → KYC/AML → Apply for contributor equity → AIPP Agreement

### Investor Route:

Investor → Capital investment (Reg D/S/CF/A) → KYC/AML & accreditation → Stock Purchase Agreement with token perk → Equity

**Token Locking as Identity Infrastructure:** When token holders voluntarily lock their tokens, they gain eligibility to apply for equity—but locking creates no automatic right, no option to purchase, and no enforceable claim. This enables cryptographic identity for shareholder voting and dividend distributions, commitment signaling that demonstrates alignment, and eligibility to petition for equity through compliant pathways.
