**Key Characteristics:**

*   **Business Operations:** All commercial activity flows through the LLC
*   **Treasury Management:** Holds and manages VITA-FAST funds (derived from IP-NFT fractionalization and token sales)
*   **IP:** Perpetual, worldwide, sublicensable license to develop and commercialize research IP arising from the Newcastle autophagy program, with full ownership of developed products.
*   **Equity Issuance:** Can issue equity via Reg D/S/CF/A + Rule 701 compliant pathways
*   **QSBS Eligible:** Potential tax benefits for qualifying shareholders

**LLC Responsibilities:**

*   Execute VITA-FAST governance decisions relating to treasury operations
*   Sublicense research IP to development partners and commercialization counterparties under perpetual worldwide license from VitaDAO
*   Enter contracts with Newcastle University, Korolchuk Lab, and partners
*   Execute sponsored research agreement addendums
*   Pursue equity financing from strategic investors
*   Handle regulatory compliance, tax obligations, and reporting
*   Manage day-to-day operations for research advancement and commercialization

**Management Structure:** Neuro Longevity LLC, which is managed by Oliver Armitage (a core lead for the VITA-FAST project), will be designated as the sole manager of VitaFast LLC.

This structure preserves token-holder governance through the DUNA while ensuring the LLC operates under clear, traditional manager control for legal, tax, and commercial purposes.
