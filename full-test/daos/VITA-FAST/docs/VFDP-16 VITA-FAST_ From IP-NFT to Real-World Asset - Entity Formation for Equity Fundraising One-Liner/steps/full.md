# VFDP-16 VITA-FAST: From IP-NFT to Real-World Asset — Entity Formation for Equity Fundraising

## One-Liner

Establish a dual-entity structure for VITA-FAST comprising (1) VitaFast DUNA as the community governance layer with membership for VITA-FAST token holders conferred by operation of the Charter and Association Agreement of VitaFast DUNA and (2) VitaFast LLC as the operating company, enabling compliant equity financing, IP commercialization, and streamlined operations while preserving decentralized token holder governance.

## Teaser

This proposal implements VitaDAO's Coin-to-Company (C2C) model for VITA-FAST through categorical separation of community membership (DUNA) and business operations (LLC). The VitaFast DUNA provides liability protection and governance coordination for VITA-FAST token holders (who are automatically considered members of the VitaFast DUNA for so long as they remain token holders. The DUNA does not require identity verification for governance-only participation), while VitaFast LLC—holds treasury funds, sublicenses research IP, and enables equity financing for the Korolchuk Lab's autophagy research commercialization.

The DUNA will also enter into an agreement with a third party (yet to be defined) to act as its ministerial agent for limited operational and clerical functions, ensuring the DUNA can take limited actions in the real world without centralizing control.

## Details

### Motivation

**The Challenge:** Token-based projects face a fundamental tension: how to enable broad community participation and decentralized governance without triggering securities compliance obligations, while still creating pathways for real value realization. Previous approaches—ICOs, SAFTs, and ‘sufficient decentralization’ theories—have proven inadequate.

**The C2C Solution:** The Coin-to-Company model solves this through categorical separation:

* Tokens = Utility instruments for community membership and governance
* Equity = Securities issued separately through compliant exemptions
* Dual participation: The same person can be both DAO member AND shareholder through distinct pathways

**Regulatory Context:** Wyoming enacted the DUNA Act (effective July 1, 2024), providing purpose-built legal recognition for DAOs. Combined with Delaware’s established LLC framework, this enables a robust dual-entity structure that:

* Provides legal existence and liability protection
* Enables entity-level taxation (avoiding pass-through complexity)
* Permits for-profit activities while maintaining nonprofit governance layer

**Commercial Imperative:** Current market dynamics favor equity-based financing in the U.S. for strategic capital in biomedical research. The dual-entity structure positions VITA-FAST to pursue equity financing from strategic investors, pharmaceutical companies, and longevity-focused funds while maintaining community governance.

## Specification

### 1. VitaFast DUNA (Community Layer)

**Purpose:** The DUNA serves as the community coordination layer—a legally recognized wrapper that provides liability protection for token holders while enabling decentralized governance.

**Key Characteristics:**

*   **Liability Shield:** Members protected from personal liability for DUNA actions
*   **Membership:** Conferred by operation of the Charter and Association Agreement upon qualifying participation (e.g., holding the VITA-FAST governance token). No separate application is required.
*   **Governance Rights:** Token holders may vote on community matters via Snapshot
*   **No Treasury:** DUNA does not hold funds—avoids securities classification concerns
*   **Ministerial Agent:** Agreement will be entered into with a third party (yet to be defined) purely for ministerial services (e.g., appointing counsel, disbursing approved funds, record-keeping, reporting) under DUNA oversight.

**DUNA Responsibilities:**

*   Coordinate governance processes and community decision-making
*   Provide formal membership structure for VITA-FAST token holders (automatic upon token holding)
*   Execute governance decisions that don't require treasury operations
*   Maintain alignment between community governance and LLC operations
*   Report to token holders on governance matters

### 2. VitaFast LLC (Operating Layer)

**Purpose:** VitaFast LLC is the operating company—conducting all business activities, holding license to IP and treasury, generating revenue, and enabling equity issuance through compliant securities exemptions.

**Key Characteristics:**

*   **Business Operations:** All commercial activity flows through the LLC
*   **Treasury Management:** Holds and manages VITA-FAST funds (derived from IP-NFT fractionalization and token sales)
*   **IP:** Perpetual, worldwide, sublicensable license to develop and commercialize research IP arising from the Newcastle autophagy program, with full ownership of developed products.
*   **Equity Issuance:** Can issue equity via Reg D/S/CF/A + Rule 701 compliant pathways
*   **QSBS Eligible:** Potential tax benefits for qualifying shareholders

**LLC Responsibilities:**

*   Execute VITA-FAST governance decisions relating to treasury operations
*   Sublicense research IP to development partners and commercialization counterparties under perpetual worldwide license from VitaDAO
*   Enter contracts with Newcastle University, Korolchuk Lab, and partners
*   Execute sponsored research agreement addendums
*   Pursue equity financing from strategic investors
*   Handle regulatory compliance, tax obligations, and reporting
*   Manage day-to-day operations for research advancement and commercialization

**Management Structure:** Neuro Longevity LLC, which is managed by Oliver Armitage (a core lead for the VITA-FAST project), will be designated as the sole manager of VitaFast LLC.

This structure preserves token-holder governance through the DUNA while ensuring the LLC operates under clear, traditional manager control for legal, tax, and commercial purposes.

**Categorical Separation:** The DUNA and LLC have distinct legal purposes and rights. This separation ensures:

*   **DUNA = Membership:** Token holders are DAO members with governance voice but no automatic equity rights
*   **LLC = Ownership:** Shareholders hold equity with economic rights via separate agreements
*   **Tokens ≠ Securities:** Utility/commodity classification maintained
*   **Equity = Securities:** Issued only through compliant exemptions

## 4. Treasury Transfer to LLC

Transfer VITA-FAST treasury funds to VitaFast LLC:

*   Funds derived from IP-NFT fractionalization and token sales
*   LLC treasury enables traditional banking relationships for operational needs
*   On-chain transparency maintained through regular reporting
*   Funds remain dedicated to VITA-FAST purposes per Whitepaper and Assignment Agreements

## 5. Equity Financing Pathways

The LLC structure enables compliant equity financing through two pathways:

### Community Member Route:

Token holder → Voluntary token lock → KYC/AML → Apply for contributor equity → AIPP Agreement

### Investor Route:

Investor → Capital investment (Reg D/S/CF/A) → KYC/AML & accreditation → Stock Purchase Agreement with token perk → Equity

**Token Locking as Identity Infrastructure:** When token holders voluntarily lock their tokens, they gain eligibility to apply for equity—but locking creates no automatic right, no option to purchase, and no enforceable claim. This enables cryptographic identity for shareholder voting and dividend distributions, commitment signaling that demonstrates alignment, and eligibility to petition for equity through compliant pathways.

*   **Decentralization Preserved:** Token holder governance remains supreme via DUNA coordination
