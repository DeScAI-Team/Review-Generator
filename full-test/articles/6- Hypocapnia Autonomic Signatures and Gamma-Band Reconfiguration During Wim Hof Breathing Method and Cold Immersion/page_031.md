Dots are individuals; boxes = IQR with median; whiskers = 1.5× IQR.

Across phases, both groups show the canonical WHM shift (↓EtCO₂, ↓SpO₂, ↑pulse, stabilised RR), but the experienced (L2) cohort displays a lower EtCO₂ and higher Pulse in L2 (not statistically significant).
